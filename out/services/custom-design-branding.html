Our Design Process

1

Initial Consultation

We begin by understanding your brand, industry, requirements, and design preferences through in-depth conversations with stakeholders.

2

Research & Concept Development

Our design team researches industry standards, cultural considerations, and current trends to create initial concept boards and design directions.

3

Design Presentation

We present multiple design concepts with detailed illustrations, fabric swatches, and color palettes for your review and feedback.

4

Refinement & Prototyping

Based on your feedback, we refine the chosen designs and create prototypes for physical evaluation of fit, comfort, and appearance.

5

Final Production

After prototype approval, we proceed with full production, implementing strict quality control measures throughout the manufacturing process.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the custom design process take?

The timeline varies based on project complexity, but typical custom design projects take 4-6 weeks from initial consultation to final design approval. After approval, production time depends on quantity and specifics but generally ranges from 4-8 weeks. We can accommodate rush projects when possible.

Do you charge for the initial design consultation?

We offer a complimentary initial consultation to understand your needs and project scope. If you decide to proceed with our design services, we charge a design fee that is later credited toward your production order upon approval.

Can you match specific brand colors exactly?

Yes, we use the Pantone Matching System (PMS) to ensure precise color matching to your brand guidelines. We provide fabric swatches for approval before production to confirm color accuracy.

What information do you need to start the design process?

To begin, we need your brand guidelines, any existing uniform specifications, details about staff roles and working environments, design preferences, and budget parameters. The more information you provide, the more targeted our initial designs will be.

Can you create designs for all departments in our organization?

Absolutely. We specialize in creating cohesive uniform collections that accommodate different roles while maintaining brand consistency. From executives to front-line staff, we ensure all uniforms work together as part of a unified program.
